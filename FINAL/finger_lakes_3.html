Arts & Culture

The Finger Lakes host a variety of arts and cultures through it's history. From Mark Twain, the Iriquois Tribe to performing arts, glass manufacuturing, salt mining, and rocks there are many cultures to experience. Here are some places where culture and arts thrive and where you can learn more about:

  • Corning Museum of Glass
  • The Rockwell Museum
  • Corning's Gaffer District
  • Mark Twain Country
  • Chemung Valley History Museum
