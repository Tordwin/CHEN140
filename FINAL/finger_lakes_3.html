Activities

There are many activities for any kind of traveler to enjoy at the Finger Lakes. Here is a list of things you can do to enjoy your time here!

Wine Tasting:

  • Seneca Lake Wine Tasting & Tour
  • Seneca Lake South Wine Tastings Tour

Tours:

  • Canandaigua Lake-Groups or Individuals Sunset Tour
  • Corning Museum of Glass

Finger Lake Parks:

  • Watkins Glen State Park
  • Buttermilk Falls State Park
  • Keuka Lake

    • At these parks you can partake in:

    • Hiking
    • Fishing
    • Hunting
    • Horseback riding
    • Biking
    • Camping
    • Skiing and Snowboarding (Winter)
    • Snowmobiling (Winter)
